Scribe

Brand Identity & Design System

01. Naming Strategy

Recommended

Scribe

Professional, timeless, and literal. It implies an intelligent assistant that writes for you. High trust factor, fits "knowledge workers" perfectly.

Cue

Short, punchy, action-oriented. "Right on cue." Implies the start of speech or a memory trigger.

Trace

Subtle and poetic. Leaving a trace of your thoughts. Privacy-conscious vibe (trace, but only locally).

Hark

Old English for "Listen." Unique, short, and memorable. Stands out against techy names.

Vera

Latin for "Truth." Emphasizes the accuracy of the verbatim transcript.

02. Logo Concepts

The Sound Block

Minimalist macOS-style app icon. Represents a sound wave captured within a solid, trustworthy container.

The Abstract Pen

Combines a fountain pen nib with a waveform at the bottom. Suggests transcription.

The Capture Ring

Focuses on the "recording" aspect. A red recording dot surrounded by a data/processing ring.

05. Voice & Tone

Personality

Professional but Personal. We are the smart colleague who takes great notes. We aren't a faceless corporation.

Transparent. We talk openly about data privacy and API keys.

Concise. We don't use fluff. Our users are busy.

Guidelines

Say "Connect your API key"
Say "Stored locally on your Mac"
Use active voice
Say "Seamless AI integration experiences"
Say "We value your privacy" (Show, don't say)
Use excessive jargon
